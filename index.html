Hello! This is Yang Yang. I'm an undergraduate student at Sichuan University, majoring in Mechanics, and a full-time Research Assistant at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Hongliang Ren. I was also fortunate to work with Professors Wenbo Ding, Daolin Ma and Hong Zhang.


My research interests are in Robotic Perception and Manipulation, Deep Learning and Mechanics. They encompass tactile sensing, contact mechanics and dexterous manipulation. I am particularly interested in the design of tactile sensors and the application of tactile sensing to enhance robotic dexterous manipulation and surgical procedures.

My goal is to push robots to approach or even exceed human-level sensing capabilities, and finally achieve human-level dexterity.

I am actively looking for a PhD position starting in Fall 2025. Please contact me if you have any leads!
